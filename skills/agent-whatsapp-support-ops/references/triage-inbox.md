# Triage Inbox Messages

## What Success Looks Like

You convert raw WhatsApp message threads into a clean triage view with:
- Intent classification for each thread.
- Priority level with rationale.
- Risk flags (policy, legal, abuse, security, billing sensitivity).
- Recommended owner and next action.

The output is immediately usable by a support team without additional interpretation.

## Your Approach

Use a compact, auditable structure. Distinguish known facts from assumptions. Prioritize customer harm, SLA breach risk, and business impact when assigning urgency.

## Output Format

Provide a table with:
- Conversation ID or label
- Primary intent
- Priority (P1/P2/P3)
- Risk flags
- Suggested owner
- Next action
- Confidence (High/Medium/Low)
