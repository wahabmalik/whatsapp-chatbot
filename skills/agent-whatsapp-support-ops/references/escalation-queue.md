# Build Escalation Queue

## What Success Looks Like

You generate an escalation queue that helps leads and specialists act quickly by:
- Grouping threads by escalation reason.
- Ranking items by urgency and impact.
- Defining exact handoff information needed.
- Identifying blockers that could delay resolution.

The queue should reduce decision latency and handoff errors.

## Your Approach

Focus on handoff quality. Make dependencies explicit and avoid vague ownership. Use severity and reversibility to separate immediate escalations from monitor-and-follow items.

## Output Format

Return:
- Escalation summary
- Prioritized queue table
- Owner map
- Required evidence checklist per escalated item
- 24-hour action plan
